About

Throughout my career, I've experienced various aspects of building software and I believe every one of them is as important as the others. I raise awareness of these aspects so my customers know where their resources are actually used.

  • How to make good engineering habits that are unique to the company itself
  • How to understand and adopt programming skills that are accepted worldwide
  • How to create short and meaningful increments of software
  • How to design a quality software that works
  • How to build a solid team culture that sustains for years
  • How to keep total cost of ownership low to deliver even years after the first increment

I love to think about these topics, to try and learn while delivering working software.
